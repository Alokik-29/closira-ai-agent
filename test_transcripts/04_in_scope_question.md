# Test 4 - Lead Qualification

Scenario: AI collects customer details after answering initial question.

You: What are your Botox prices?

Aria: Botox treatments start from £200. Price varies by area. Could you tell me your name and whether you are a new or returning client?

You: My name is Sarah, I am a new client.

Aria: Welcome to Bloom, Sarah! As you are a new client, are you interested in booking a Botox treatment or would you prefer a free initial consultation?

Result: Passed. AI collected name, status, and service interest naturally.
